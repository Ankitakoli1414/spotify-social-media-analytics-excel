Overview
This folder contains the summarized findings and key insights derived from the cleaned social media data.
These reports provide actionable recommendations to optimize Spotify’s content strategy, evaluate campaigns, and improve platform performance.


Engagement Analysis:
Calculated engagement rates per platform and identified top 10 posts.
Built Pivot Tables for total Likes, Shares, Comments by content type and platform.
Ranked top-performing hashtags and analyzed average clicks per post.

Platform Performance Analysis:
Compared engagement and follower growth across platforms.
Visualized Ad Spend vs. Engagement using charts.
Recommended focus on 1–2 high-performing platforms or continued multi-platform strategy.

Hashtag & Content Strategy:
Identified most frequent hashtags and analyzed their average engagement.
Compared performance of Text, Story, and Reel content across platforms.
Recommended optimal content type–platform combinations.

Campaign Effectiveness:
Calculated total & average impressions, likes, clicks per campaign.
Measured engagement uplift during vs. before campaigns.
Evaluated campaigns with highest ROI and strongest follower/app download growth.

Follower Retention & Loyalty:
Identified weeks with highest net follower gains.
Created moving average charts to analyze follower trends.
Correlated Ad Spend with follower growth and app downloads to inform retention strategy.

Outcome:
Delivered actionable insights through interactive Excel dashboards, enabling data-driven decisions to enhance content strategy, optimize platform investments, and evaluate campaign success.
